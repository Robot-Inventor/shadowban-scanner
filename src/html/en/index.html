Features of Shadowban Scanner

person

Detect shadowbans and sensitive flags on accounts

Check to see if your Twitter account has been shadowbanned. You can also check to see if your account has been sensitive flagged. You can check both your account and the accounts of others.

speaker_notes

Detect sensitive flags and age restrictions in tweets

Check to see if the tweet has been judged sensitive. You can also check if the tweet is age-restricted.

photo_library

Detect sensitive flags for profile media

You can check the media in your Twitter account profile for sensitive judgments.

south_america

Detect countries blocking your account

Some countries may block accounts for legal reasons. You can check to see if and which countries have blocked your account.

visibility_off

Add button to show hidden tweets

Twitter may hide some tweets that contain external links. Add a button to show hidden tweets.

cloud_off

No access to external sites

Shadowban Scanner displays results directly in your tweets and profiles. There is no need to navigate to an external site. It also does not access external servers or Twitter's internal API. Your privacy and data are protected.

code

Open source

Source code is available on GitHub. All features are completely free.
